What is plagiarism?

Plagiarism is the act of using someone else's work, ideas, or intellectual property without giving proper credit.

Why is it important to avoid plagiarism?

Avoiding plagiarism is crucial to maintain academic integrity and intellectual honesty. It ensures that credit is given where it's due.
